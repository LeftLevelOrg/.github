## Decentralized Privacy — Built to Stay Online  
### Communication that Never Goes Dark  

**LeftLevel** is an open initiative to design a resilient, decentralized communication framework that stays online under failure, censorship, or disconnection.  
It merges peer-to-peer networking, end-to-end encryption, and self-sovereign identity into a unified privacy layer.
